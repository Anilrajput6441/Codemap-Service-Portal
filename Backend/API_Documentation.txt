GET request:

/api/v1/technologies/ - Get List of all technologies
/api/v1/services/ - Get a list of all services
/api/v1/queries/ - Get a list of all queries
/api/v1/qoutes/ - Get a list of all  qoutes


POST request:
/api/v1/technologies/ -Post a specific technology
/api/v1/services/ -Post a specific service
/api/v1/queries/ -Post a specific query
/api/v1/qoutes/ -Post a specific qoutes



PATCH request:
/api/v1/technologies/ - Update a specific technology
/api/v1/services/ -Update a specific service


DELETE request:
/api/v1/technologies/ -Delete a specific technology
/api/v1/services/ -Delete a specific service
